01 — Why Renewal?

WE OUTGREW
OUR ROOTS.

TEP was founded in 2003. Over two decades, we flourished — building Urth, Valsora, deep Government gameplay. Each became its own culture.

Growth came at a cost. We fragmented into distinct subcommunities with separate norms, tools, and onboarding paths. New members hit a wall of confusing channels, competing identities, and diluted collective strength. Disunity became the default.

Objective
Foster a cohesive, integrated community that leverages our collective creativity to build something bigger than any one subcommunity — Tep Worlds.
