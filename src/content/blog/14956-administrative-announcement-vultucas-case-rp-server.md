The Administrative Team of The East Pacific, after thoughtful deliberations and a review of the evidence from at hand (between 13th and 16th of September 2021) and the OOC Panel being held (between 16th and 18th of September 2021), hereby announces the following conclusions:

Vultuca is indefinitely banned from all of The East Pacific official places, following:

1.1) Review of violations within RP Server from first half of 2020;

1.2.) New violation of rules within RP Server on 19th of September 2021.
- Two members of Forum Roleplay have been warned for violations that happened within 2020;

- One RP Discord Staffer has been warned for a violation that happened within the second half of 2020;

- Forum RP Staff will be reprimanded and talked to regarding healthy server moderation.
