After a review of the facts of each case, the Administrative Team declares that Rosartemis and Improper Classifications have been banned from the East Pacific for a period of approximately 6 months - i.e. until July 1st 2025.

This ban is due to facilitating the doxxing of an individual, although it is noted neither Rosartemis or Improper Classifications participated in the original doxxing event. Any repeat of this behavior during or after the temporary ban will lead to a permanent ban.

The Team acknowledges and appreciates the apologies independently made by Rosartemis and Improper Classifications on this matter. However, actions like these, even if apologized for, must still face consequences.

Questions and comments on the matter should be directed towards East Malaysia (Nation: East Malaysia, Discord: @.emjay).
