A few days ago as I was trying to track down a shadow topic issue in the Archives: Magisterium subforum of Capitol Records, I mistakenly deleted the subforum and its subforums. This included the 2014 Constitutional Convention, Magisterium Past Elections and various other topics. I immediately opened a ticket with Tapatalk but they have informed me that there is nothing they can do to recover the data.

I am deeply saddened to have to report this unfortunate news to the community. I apologize for causing this data loss and will make sure it does not happen again.

To ensure this does not happen again I will make sure to remove all subforums from any subforums that have the shadow topic issue.

If you have any questions feel free to message me on discord. I can answer in more detail there.
