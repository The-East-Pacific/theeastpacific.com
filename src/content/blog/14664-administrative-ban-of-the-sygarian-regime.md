Administrative Ban of The Sygarian Regime

On 05/11/2021 The Sygarian Regime was temporarily banned from The East Pacific Roleplay server due to a video that was posted that in the voice chat text channel. This video was a little over 3 minutes long of “spicy facts” (in the video description) about BLM and George Floyd. It was in extremely poor taste upon roleplay server moderative staff review of the video. This was issued by the Roleplay Moderator after staff discussion as The Sygarian Regime was already on a final warning from January of this year. The warning was issued after The Sygarian Regime posted a meme/video in the meme channel of the roleplay server, on Martin Luther King, Jr. Day, with the caption of “In 500 ft. turn right onto Martin Luther King Jr. Blvd” with a 20 second clip of an active war zone.

 

Standard operating procedure within the roleplay server is to announce to the entire server of any bans, mutes, etc. as it could affect other roleplays. After this announcement was made, a user did a search and seen that The Sygarian Regime had posted two memes in the voice text channel of The East Pacific server in February of 2020. The memes are simply inappropriate in a sexual nature and will not be described. The ban has been made permanent from all The East Pacific.

-The East Pacific Administrative Team
